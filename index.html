Frequently Asked Questions

What internet speeds do you offer?

We offer various speed packages ranging from 10 Mbps for basic home use to 100 Mbps for enterprise solutions. All packages include unlimited data.

How long does installation take?

Standard installation typically takes 2-4 hours. Our technicians will schedule a convenient time and ensure everything is working perfectly before leaving.

Do you provide 24/7 customer support?

Our support team is here for you every day from 8:00 AM to midnight via phone. For on-site or specialized assistance, our skilled technicians are available from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM to keep you connected and running smoothly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa payment through our paybill

Is there a contract period?

We offer flexible plans with no long-term contracts for home users. Business packages may have optional contract terms with better rates.
